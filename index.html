Coqui Reca is London based multi instrumentalist. Originally from Argentina, Coqui Reca, A.K.A George, has been in London since the early 1990's having previously lived in Zurich.
In the 1980's Coqui was the lead singer and guitarist of El Calefon who performed all over Europe. Later, in the 1990's he joined the London based band Canfusion.
